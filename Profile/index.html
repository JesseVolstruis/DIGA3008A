Point Perfect

Prototype

A playable prototype for a 2D precision platformer inspired by Celeste. Choose platforming levels from the select screen and make it to the end, one screen at a time. Execute your jumps and dashes to make it through each level. Developed solo.

Psuedo

Game jam

An analogue social deduction game for 4-8 players. Players answer prompts while acting out hilarious scenarios. Meanwhile, a randomly selected Deceiver tries to fool their friends. Won best analogue for Campus Game Jam 2022. Worked on gameplay design.

Air Hockey

Course assignment

A physics simulation game of air hockey. Use your mouse to drag the puck to score 5 points against your AI opponent, score trick shots with the arrow booster pads, and most importantly have fun :) Made solo for a course assignment.

90s Twist

Course assignment

An abstract strategy game for 2 players with a chill 90s inspired aesthetic. Players take turns rotating their boards and placing their pieces with the goal of lining them up. Enjoy the relaxing vibes and art direction. Made for a course assignment in a group of 5. Worked as a programmer.

Chop Shop

Course assignment

A UI/Inventory display featuring a sword shop. Browse the wares of the slightly disheveled looking shopkeep. Buy or sell swords, stash them in your Inventory, or simply admire the thoughtful flavor text. Assets are hand drawn if you're wondering why they look like that. Made solo for a course assignment.

Split

Prototype

A split-screen, co-op, top-down puzzle game for 2 players. Each player can interact with the environment slighlty differently. Work together to get to the end of this playable prototype. Developed solo.
