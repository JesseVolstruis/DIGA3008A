Site content and elements:

For my site's content, I still haven't progressed passed my idea for a simple website. The navigation will most likely be very simple with some flashier styling to add some flavour and keep user's interest while using the site. Starting at the homepage, I expect this page to be the most enticing design wise as it's the first thing users will see when visiting the site. As for the design itself, I haven't thought of what exactly it should be. It will most likely relate to myself in some way, but I suspect it will have to do with games as it's my hobby and desired career path. Below the banner for the home page will most likely be a menu with all the sections of the site to choose from. (Blogs, design etc.) For blogs, it will most likely be a column of the blog titles that users will scroll through. Clicking on a blog title will expand it to the full blog post. As for the other sections, I don't have any concrete ideas for those yet and most likely won't until I have progressed enough. My profile section will most likely be a profile picture with important links and a description. Each page will have some way of navigating to any page on the site. My initial plan for this is buttons at the bottom of each of each page that can be clicked to navigate to any desired page, that way once the user is finished reading a page they can easily navigate to the next.

Folder structure:

The content above roughly lines up with my current folder structure. Although not much is currently integrated. Each page for the website has its own scripts, styles and images folder. I don't expect much to change until I am far more experienced with CSS and Javascript.

Progress:

In terms of progress, my website now has multiple pages that are linked via hyperlinks. The home page has a header and some navigation in the form of 4 links to the other pages. As of now, only the blogs link functions and has its respective page. I haven't added any of the blogs themselves to the page yet, as I was planning to do this once at a later date. I will be doing this sooner rather than later to get a feel for how I want the actual design of the page to be later. Currently there is an image and video on the site, although these are placeholders and are just to practice inserting images and videos onto the site and will be replaced with more relevant content as time goes on. I have also cleaned up my folder structure as I prepare to add even more pages and eventually start using scripts and styles. Each page of the site has its own folder, with each folder having its styles scripts etc.
