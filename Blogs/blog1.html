Github:

Although I have a small bit of prior experience with Github, I am not familiar enough with how it works currently. Setting it up and publishing to pages was fairly simple and I'm excited for how my experience with Github will progress as I get more comfortable using it. It seems very useful, especially since I'm interested in game development. In order to increase my efficiency with collaborative coding and programming in general, it's best that I put in the proper effort to understand how Github works.

Readings:

The World-Wide Web By Berners-Lee et al.

Reading this article gave me insight into the earlier developments of the world wide web as we know it. The web and its sites have become deeply ingrained in a lot of society and some people, myself included, can so easily take for granted how accessible the web and its information is. It's fascinating to me that despite how complex the Web is, it is so simple to navigate. In its early development the web seemed to be mostly focused on compiling human knowledge. The original vision for the web was for scientists to share documents after all. However, the web has grown significantly since then. Today, while also hosting a vast amount of human knowledge, the web is also used as sources of entertainment and communication. The web has grown from being knowledge-centric to being multipurpose and being relevant in many aspects of modern life. It's fascinating seeing the developments that were highly anticipated of the web in its early days, and seeing how many we have not only achieved, but surpassed, showing how we have advanced since the webs creation. The internet has greatly influenced my life, seeing as I use it very often for communication, education, and most predominantly entertainment. The dot com bubble While the dot-com bubble in many ways shaped the internet we experience today, I notice some parallels between this dot-com bubble and other bubbles we encounter online today such tech startups and crypto currencies. With crypto currency specifically I notice a similar focus on high valuations and hype-based cycles. The dot-com bubble also seems to have paved the way for tech giants such as Google and Amazon partly due to how they learned from the mistakes made during the dot-com era by focusing on sustainability rather than short term hype.

Flash:

This was an interesting read considering how little I had known about Flash considering their past relevance on the internet and how ingrained into my life the internet is. It's fascinating seeing how the decline of Flash has led to an overall increase in security, performance and compatibility on the internet.

Goals for this course:

I would say my goals are to properly familiarize myself with Github. Since I plan on going into game development, collaborative coding is one of many things Github will help me with. Getting accustomed to it should benefit me. I also want to know more about the web, seeing as I spend a large amount of time on the internet and that it greatly impacts my life. I also want to become proficient with HTML and or Javascript as I think being proficient with them could be very beneficial for my future.
